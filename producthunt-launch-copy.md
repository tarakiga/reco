## First comment (post as the maker, right after launch)
Hey Product Hunt 👋

I built Haystackk because I kept losing track of what I actually wanted to watch — scattered across screenshots, texts to friends, and three different streaming apps' half-broken watchlists.

Haystackk is a single, fast place to:
- Search any movie or show and see the cast, trailer, and where it's actually streaming, renting, or available to buy (powered by JustWatch/TMDB data)
- Build a watchlist with status (want to watch / watching / watched)
- Rate what you've seen with a simple 5-star scale
- Browse by genre and year when you don't know what you want yet

This is v1 — the catalog and watchlist foundation. Next up: reviews and shared lists with friends, and an AI taste layer that actually explains *why* it's recommending something to you (not just "because you watched X").

Would love your feedback, especially on what's missing from your own watchlist habit. Thanks for checking it out!
